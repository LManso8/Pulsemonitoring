# Environmental Standards

## NP 2074

### Purpose

Assessment of environmental vibrations and their impact on structures.

### Application

Environmental Mode.

### Evaluated Parameters

- Peak Particle Velocity (PPV)
- Frequency

---

## DIN 4150

### Purpose

Assessment of structural damage caused by vibrations.

### Application

Environmental Mode.

### Evaluated Parameters

- Peak Particle Velocity (PPV)
- Frequency
- Structural vibration limits

---

## Usage in PULSE

The Environmental Mode compares measured vibration levels with the limits defined by these standards and determines compliance.
